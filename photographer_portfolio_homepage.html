The photographer

Light as a
confession

I work at the threshold of visibility — in the hour before dawn, in rooms where shadows hold more truth than objects. My practice is rooted in stillness, and in the belief that the most honest moments are the ones nobody planned for.

Based between Mumbai and Tokyo, I work with editorial clients, private collectors, and cultural institutions who understand that beauty is not decorative — it is essential.

"Every frame is a negotiation with time."
